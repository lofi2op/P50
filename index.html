Interesting Facts About Pigeons

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  2. Pigeons are renowed for their outstanding navigational abilities
  3. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities

Interesting Facts About Pandas

  1. The typical panda grows from 1.2 m to 1.9 m
  2. Pandas are native to south-central China
  3. An estimated 1,864 pandas still roam the wild, while 400 are kept in captivity

Interesting Facts About Chameleons

  1. Their feet work like Saland Tongs
  2. Almost half of all known species live in Madagascar
  3. They mainly change color in order to communicate or regulate body temperature

Interesting Facts About Cicadas

  1. Cicadas has one of the longest insect lifespans
  2. Cicadas inundate forests as a survival mechanism
  3. They can buzz louder than a lawnmower
